Hamburger

Description

This simple recipe makes delicious hamburgers, ideal for serving 4 people. Adjust the quantities as needed for your meal.

Ingredients

  • 600 g of ground beef
  • 4 brioche buns
  • 2 large tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 150 g of cheddar cheese
  • 4 large lettuce leaves
  • Truffle mayonnaise, to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare the patties:
    1. Divide the ground beef into 4 portions.
    2. Shape each portion into a round patty.
    3. Season each patty with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook the patties until desired doneness is reached.
  3. Place a slice of cheddar cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking.
  4. Toast the buns until golden brown.
  5. Assemble the burgers:
      • Place the cooked patties on the bottom half of each bun.
      • Top with lettuce, tomato slices, and a dollop of truffle mayonnaise.
      • Cover with the top half of the bun.
