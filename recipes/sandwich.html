Sandwich

Description

This is a simple but delicious sandwich that fits great as breakfast or a late night snack. With just a few ingredients you get the energy your body needs in just a few minutes.

Ingridients

  • Bread of choice
  • Ham
  • Tomato
  • Cream cheese
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. Cut the bread in half
  2. Put cream cheese on both sides
  3. Slice the Tomato in thin pieces
  4. Put the tomato slices on the bottom
  5. Slice the avocado in thin pieces
  6. Put the slices of avocado above the tomatoes
  7. Put the ham at the top
  8. Put the second half of bread over the bottom one
